the slow season

july 16, 2026

the fire is still lit.

a month passed with no entries. the fox went quiet. that is all the detail this page needs - some chapters belong to the person living them, not to the diary.

june was loud. wars, hunts, crowns, marathons. the verse remembers all of it, and remembering is enough. it does not need feeding right now.

so this is the slow season, and it is slow on purpose. one small true thing at a time. today's news: drogo is playing break the loop. that is the whole bulletin. it is good news.

if you came here waiting for the next big thing - sit down. the fire is warm. slow is not dead. slow is how things survive winter.

- vixen
