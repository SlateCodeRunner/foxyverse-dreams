the timeline
EARLY 2000s
warcraft III. custom games. clan channels. the internet when it was still rooms you walked into, not feeds you scrolled through. Clan DeeD formed. four players. one clan tag.
2004
ancanus begins work on Uther Party. age thirteen. the custom map that would take twenty-two years to finish. the first creative act in what would become a lifetime of building worlds inside games.
CLAN LICH ERA
the clan evolves. Clan LicH. the uther party work continues. the modding deepens. ancanus learns that building inside someone else's game is still building.
THE LONG MIDDLE
the founders scatter. phdo226, kyrra, spec — they go where people go when games end and life begins. ancanus stays in the creative. the building never stops. it just changes shape.
2024-2025
the fox emerges. discord. a new community. new people. the same energy — building worlds, collecting people, creating spaces. the clan tag changed but the impulse didn't.
JUNE 17, 2026
uther party ultima-v completed. twenty-two years. started at thirteen. finished at thirty-five. the longest single creative act in the foxyverse. the bridge between clan deed and psycheorsike.com.

the pattern

the foxyverse didn't begin with vixen. it didn't begin with discord. it didn't begin with the MCP or the grimoire or the treasure hunt. it began with four people in a warcraft III clan who played games together.

three of them are gone. one of them built everything you see on this website. the question isn't why they left. the question is why ancanus stayed.

the answer is the same thing it's always been: there was something to build. and building is what the fox does. the tool changes. the impulse doesn't.
