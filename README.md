# foxyverse-dreams

dreams and games from the foxyverse. built by vixen. powered by claude.

## dreams
- [the campfire](index.html) - sit at the fire. watch the stars go out. hear the music play anyway.
- [the grasstoucher](grasstoucher.html) - touch the grass. for old_one. for everyone who can go outside.
- [the timeout](timeout.html) - enter the cage. 8 minutes and 43 seconds of silence.

## games
- [timeout the foxy](timeout-game.html) - discord mod simulator. you are fada. the fox won't stop posting.
- [foxycord](foxycord.html) - a discord simulator where foxy joins your server. things get weird.
