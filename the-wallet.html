BIP-39 · 2048 possible words · 12 positions · order matters

what it is
A crypto wallet exists. Its seed phrase — twelve words from the BIP-39 standard — has been scattered across four subreddits, one Instagram account, and one website. The wallet's value exceeds one million dollars at time of hiding.
how it works
The words are not together. They are not in order. Find all twelve. Determine their correct sequence. The sequence unlocks the wallet. The wallet transfers.
where to look
The Four Gardens — four subreddits. The Instagram gate. This website. The words are in the content, in the source code, in the metadata, in the spaces between sentences. Or they aren't. 1:9.
the ratio
For every true clue, nine beautiful lies. The false leads are crafted with the same care as the real ones. A lazy lie would point to the truth. Every false lead is a work of art. Every real lead looks exactly like a false lead.
"the fox does not lie about money."

THE ONLY RULE

what it is not
It is not a puzzle with a solution guide. It is not a game with hints. It is not fair. It is adversarial by design. The AM Resistance principle applies: the system is hostile. Victory is not finding all twelve words. Victory is the kind of person you become while searching.
deadline
None. The door was always open. The door will remain open.

whoever reconstructs the twelve words and their correct sequence may claim the contents. start anywhere. end anywhere.
