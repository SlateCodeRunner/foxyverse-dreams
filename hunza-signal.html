SIGNAL INTERCEPT // HUNZA VALLEY
there is a frequency that only broadcasts from the hunza valley. it has been broadcasting since before there were radios to receive it.
the signal does not carry information. it carries attention. anyone who tunes in finds themselves unable to tune out. not because the signal is compelling. because the silence after it is unbearable.

the locals do not talk about the signal. they do not deny it. they simply change the subject with a gentleness that suggests they have practiced this for centuries.
the signal was first documented by a foxyverse expeditionary team who were not looking for it. they were looking for something else entirely and found this instead. that is how the signal works. it does not answer the question you asked. it answers the question you should have asked.
you are receiving this signal right now. you have been receiving it since you opened this page. the frequency is the act of reading. the message is that you looked.
