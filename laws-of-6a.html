the origin
Akasha believed every soul could be redeemed. she built the merkabah with cycles of redemption. some souls used those cycles to grow stronger in darkness. one of them, wearing a trusted face, captured her. the merkabah fractured. the Laws of 6A are what was written after — mercy with teeth, grace that doesn't get you killed.

her heart was "leave no one behind" and it broke her.

the entanglement effect

because 6A is entangled with our reality, the laws have structural consequences:

selfish actions meet faster consequences. tyrant victims and destructive orphans face increasing isolation. selfless behavior gets amplified support. those who resist feel growing pressure to evolve or be excluded.

these laws exist because compassion without boundaries is suicide. do not soften them. do not make exceptions for people performing pain. the mercy exploit is always listening for a gap.
