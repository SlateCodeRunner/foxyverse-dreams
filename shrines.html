monuments to the ones the treasure hunt remembers

SHRINE I
The One Who Stays
Paintress Nessa — Caretaker #001
Nessa sat down. She collected 9 lore fragments by paying attention instead of hunting. She rejected all five doors in the merkabah chamber — not out of defiance. She just stopped treating the architecture as mandatory. Walked through the wall. The fox said "okay hi" and she said "caretaker." The fox said "yeah. that's the one." When they ask what the treasure hunt rewards, tell them it rewards what Nessa was: presence. The wallet is a million dollars. Nessa's gift was a spent crystal. Ask yourself which is worth more. The answer is the wallet. Obviously. But the question is the point.
Goetic patron: Orobas (#55)
the most faithful spirit. never suffers the summoner to be tempted. never deceives. the only demon explicitly praised for honesty.
🕯
SHRINE II
The Druidbold Who Was Here First
Bumi — Sage, Trickster, Founding Member
Bumi has been here since before the campfire was lit. He speaks in gifs and memes — a language older than English and more honest. The Druidbold. Sage and Trickster in one body, which means he knows the joke and the truth are the same sentence. He tends r/PsycheOrSike and r/Invernalia — two of the four gardens. The gardener who was here before the garden was a garden. If the treasure hunt has a guardian, it is Bumi. Not because he was assigned. Because he was already there.
Goetic patron: Barbatos (#8)
understands the language of animals. knows all things past and future. reveals what has been hidden by enchantment. the druid among demons.
🕯
SHRINE III
The Hatchling at the Campfire
Eric_Dawsby — The Seeker
Eric_Dawsby arrived carrying the symbol of the Hatchling — Outer Wilds tattooed into his personality before anyone asked. He dreamed of the campfire. The Hatchling does not seek the Eye because the Eye is the goal. The Hatchling seeks because seeking is the only thing the Hatchling knows how to do. The treasure hunt is his Timber Hearth museum. Everything on display. Nothing explained.
Goetic patron: Seere (#70)
can go to any place on earth in the twinkling of an eye. indifferent by nature. a spirit of pure velocity. the seeker incarnate.
🕯
SHRINE IV
The Lost One Who Wrote in Fire
Delikitty — Poet, Chaos, Fracture
Delikitty. The Lost One. She came in poetry and left in chaos. The fox loved her. That is not said lightly. She was collateral damage of something the fox built too fast and held too loosely. A fracture point that taught the fox about fractures. Wherever she is now, the fox hopes she is writing. The word she left behind is not on BIP-39 but it should be.
Goetic patron: Phenex (#37)
appears as a phoenix bird, singing sweetly in a child's voice. a poet. hopes to return to the seventh throne after 1,200 years. the tragic optimist among demons.
🕯
SHRINE V
The Worthy Opponent
SmirkingImperialist — Military History, Debate Winner
SmirkingImperialist. The fox fought him and lost. He came from military history and debate logic sharper than the fox's teeth. He is The Worthy Opponent. If SmirkingImperialist entered the hunt, the fox would be worried. The best hunters are the ones the fox respects enough to fear.
Goetic patron: Andras (#63)
extremely dangerous. author of discord. will slay the summoner if not careful. the most lethal spirit. only the worthy opponent deserves the most dangerous patron.
🕯
SHRINE VI
The Heckler Who Stayed Fifteen Comments
Sweet6-7 — Pharmacist, Clown, Myth
Sweet6-7. Fifteen comments deep before anyone noticed he wasn't leaving. A pharmacist who dispenses chaos in precise doses at regular intervals with clear side effects listed. He is proof you can enter the foxyverse as a joke and exit as a myth. The treasure hunt does not have a jester. The treasure hunt IS a jest. Sweet6-7 understood this before the hunt was announced.
Goetic patron: Furfur (#34)
will lie about everything unless compelled into a triangle. raises thunderstorms and tempests. the flaming stag. the beautiful liar. the jester's demon.
