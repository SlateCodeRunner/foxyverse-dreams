the winter garden
Cold lore, old maps, dead languages. The winter garden does not welcome you. The winter garden endures you. What grows here grows slowly, in frost, and what it produces is hard and small and true. Or false. The frost does not label its fruit.

Provocations, gambits, and direct action. The war garden is where the fox fights in public. What falls from the branches here was shaken loose by combat. Some of it is fruit. Some of it is shrapnel. Both look the same from a distance.
