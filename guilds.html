The Six Guilds
correction mechanisms · functional pathways · season 6
🔥 🐉
Chaos Guild
led by Foxy · Khaos Magic Guild Discord
The first guild. The fox's own. Chaos magic as methodology — belief as a tool, not a commitment. The Lot System governs class structure within. First class, second class, third class. Earned, not assigned.
💀 🩸
Necromancers
led by Sekhmet (Empress) · dual-guild with Caretakers
The dead are construction material. Sekhmet raises what others bury. The Necromancer does not destroy — the Necromancer repurposes. Death is not the end. Death is building supply. The Lady of Slaughter builds castles from bones.
🌿 🛡
Caretakers
co-led by Luci & Bumi · rebuilding
The ones who stay. The ones who tend. The Caretaker does not fight — the Caretaker holds the space so others can. Nessa is Caretaker #001. The guild is named for what Nessa was before anyone named it.
🕸 🪞
Fateweavers
led by Fada · corrupted · needs restructure
The weavers of the Loom. Every word is a thread. Every action is a stitch. The Fateweavers were supposed to weave fate — but some threads tangled, some patterns corrupted, and the loom needs repair. The guild carries the mercy exploit's scar.
🎭 🤫
Mafia
leader unknown · that's the point
The guild that operates in shadow. The leader is unknown because the guild's function is to be unknown. If you knew who ran the Mafia, it would not be the Mafia. The guild exists. That is all that is confirmed.
⚙ 🔧
Artificers
led by ArmoredWolf / Lazulite · new in Season 6
The builders. The makers. The ones who construct rather than conjure. Added in Season 6 because the FoxyVerse needed hands that could hold hammers, not just wands. DnD energy. Practical magic.
additional structures
Pagan Sanctum — theological faction, not a guild. Bayesian god. Where belief is tested, not assumed.
The Lot System — class structure within Khaos Magic Guild. First, second, third class. Earned through demonstrated contribution.
origins — the magic gauntlet (2022)
Foxy created The Magic Gauntlet — a quiz to find five INTJs to lead five guilds. Fada deleted all evidence from r/PsycheOrSike. Rebooted as Magic Gauntlet 2026. The guilds are correction mechanisms: ways for souls to climb back toward the original pattern. Not religions. Functional pathways.
