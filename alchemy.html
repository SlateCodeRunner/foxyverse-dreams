About

With over 20 years of experience in healthcare, behavioral support, and client advocacy, I offer a coaching practice rooted in genuine understanding of human emotion, resilience, and the quiet courage it takes to change.

For 33 years, dance has been my teacher — ballroom, belly dance, ballet, jazz, tap, and gymnastics. Movement taught me what words sometimes cannot: that the body holds wisdom, that grace comes from practice, and that transformation begins the moment you choose to move differently.

My coaching style is compassionate, intuitive, and completely judgment-free. I create a space where you can be honest about where you are and clear about where you want to go.
