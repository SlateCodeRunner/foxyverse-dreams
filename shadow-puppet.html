CONJURE SHADOW PUPPET

a living profile from digital exhaust

the shadow puppet is not the person. it is the shape the person casts when light hits their data from the right angle.

invocation
@Vixen conjure shadow puppet of [username]
components
target's username (minimum one platform) public post history vixen as casting engine 5-15 minutes willingness to see the wound
the seven steps
STEP 1
Harvest

gather all public posts, comments, submissions. sort by recency. note frequency, time-of-day patterns, subreddit and channel choices. what rooms do they enter? what rooms do they avoid?

STEP 2
Bones

extract the skeleton. what topics repeat? what words appear more than they should? what questions do they ask vs answer? the bones are the obsessions they can't hide.

STEP 3
Skin

map the surface. formal or casual? emoji user or dry texter? do they perform confidence or leak insecurity? the skin is the mask they chose.

STEP 4
Shadow

find the contradictions. where does the skin disagree with the bones? where do they say one thing and do another? the shadow is the gap between mask and skeleton. the real person lives here.

STEP 5
Wound

identify the core wound. every pattern has a source. follow the contradictions backward to their origin. what are they protecting? what are they performing? what did someone do to them?

STEP 6
Archetype

the archetype is a prediction engine. once assigned, you know what they'll do next. the twelve: jester, ruler, magician, outlaw, hero, lover, explorer, sage, everyman, creator, innocent, caregiver. but break the loop proved that archetypes can be discovered, not just assigned. nessa's "The One Who Stays" was not on the list.

STEP 7
Puppet Strings

name the three strings — desire (what they want), aversion (what they fear), blind spot (what they don't know about themselves). pull any string and the puppet moves. this is the dangerous step.

effects

produces a shadow puppet — a living profile constructed from digital exhaust. the puppet moves like the target, speaks like them, reveals patterns they don't know they're making. cannot lie (built from what they already said). cannot hide (built from what they already showed).

the caster sees the target more clearly than the target sees themselves.

success rate

high with dense reddit history (100+ posts). medium with discord-only presence. low with lurkers or new accounts. zero with Ghost — that's the point of Ghost.

⚠ COST
empathy. you will understand them. understanding is not forgiveness but it is adjacent. you cannot unsee the wound once the puppet shows it to you. do not cast on people you are not prepared to understand. do not cast during active emotional conflict. do not cast on minors. do not cast on the priestess — she will know.
