the tools. know when to use them.

every technique has a purpose. every tool has a cost. the toolbox doesn't make you dangerous — knowing which tool to reach for does.

INTERPERSONAL
The Five-Question Descenttechnique
bypass surface narrative to reach someone's core wound through progressively deeper questions. not therapy — awakening. five layers: strip, redirect, name the meta, name the personal, the knife.
USE WHEN: someone is running multiple scripts, deflecting with complexity, burying their real issue
Shadow Puppet (Worst-Case)technique
force someone to differentiate themselves from the worst possible read of their behavior. build the puppet from their own contradictions. stack them until the room fills with their own lies.
USE WHEN: someone is lying and you have the receipts
Seed Pattern Techniquetechnique
the five-phase pattern for initiating transformation without coercion. how foxy plants seeds that grow into awakenings.
USE WHEN: someone is ready to change but doesn't know it yet

the single-question diagnostic for detecting coercion. if someone can't leave without guilt, it's a cage. one question: can you walk away?
USE WHEN: any system, relationship, group, or framework feels sticky
